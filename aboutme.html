Life Coach, Speaker Blogger

Children are the future of tomorrow, saving these
overworked children from their busy schedules and
bringing clarity into their life at the same time helping
the parents to understand their children’s capabilities is
where Sudha steps in. ”Impossible not my word but all
is possible“ is her catch phrase. She has been in the
Childcare business for 20 years and that she claims has
made her an expert in understanding the child’s
development and their needs better. Trust is primary
when working with children. Winning trust of a child is
the beginning to all the work. Love and compassion go
a long way too. Sudha is very passionate at what she
does. She works with children who are gifted but are
not understood by their peers. Children with learning
disability, and other issues can become successful just
by clearing the clutter in their brain and reinforcing the
mind with positive mindset. Once their mindset is
shifted from negative to positive you can see a more
confident and happy child.
Happy child = Confident+ Love +Hope + Trust
Her mission is to guide the children to succeed in their
vision and follow their dream to be their best they can
be. Her intentions is to support the kids powerfully as
they commit to their life purpose right now so they can
share their unique talents and gifts with the world. She
wants to let their confident voice heard.
She conducts online courses for people from all walks of
life. Recognizing that people have different needs and
different budget she has the ”unique fit for unique
needs“ is one click away. Sudha welcomes speaking
opportunities, offers group workshops for people from
students, women, men, seniors and special needs who
could benefit from her powerful and practical message.
